It is forbidden to bring food into the Library. The only drink permitted in the Library is water in a container with a non-spill cap. Mobile phones must be switched to 'silent' mode. Conversations on mobile phones must be held outside of the Library. The rule of silence must be respected and antisocial behaviour will not be tolerated. Offenders will be required to leave, and repeat offenders will be banned indefinitely from using the Library. All books and periodicals must be returned to the shelves after use.
